About Health Coaching

In the PACT program, health coaching is designed to a relationship-centered, client-driven process designed to facilitate and support you in making changes and achieve your goals in preventing chronic pain and addiction while promoting your health and well-being. Here is how you can use the health coach to support your success in this program.

 

Health Coaching

Goals of the Health Coach

In the PACT program, the health coach will help you:

Set goals and provide you with support in achieving them

Review interest, readiness for change, understanding barriers, and action plans

Facilitate change using a safe, consistent, non-judgmental,and supportive approach

Empower your own choices to engage in self-care with your intrinsic self-motivation and energy

Engaging responsibly to inspire and support you to function independently and successfully

Engaging your internal strengths and external resources to achieve sustainable changes in your thoughts, emotions, and lifestyle choices

 

Health Coaching

Steps to work with your Health Coach

After enrolling in the PACT program and completing your pain assessment, you will be introduced to your personal health coach.

You will receive an email introducing you to your health coach after you complete your initial pain assessment.

Your health coach will contact you to schedule your first telehealth coaching visit.

 

The First Visit

At the first visit, the health coach will call you

Together, you will decide how the coach can best help you improve your condition

You need to establish goals and work on how best to achieve them

Your coach will provide a safe, consistent, non-judgmental, and supportive approach at each visit

Together, you can both set up a series of additional calls in your personal PACT calendar

 

Scheduling Additional Health Coach Visits:

Your PACT program includes 8 health coach visits within the 6 month program. After 6 months, you will have the option to extend your program for a fee.

During each call, you can discuss the program, its goals, your action plan, and how you can best achieve them

You can both track your progress in the dashboard, any barriers that arise, and how best to overcome them

You can also send emails and comments directly to your health coach.
