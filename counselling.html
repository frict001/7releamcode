About Clinical Counseling

For some clients in the PACT program, they desire more in-depth clinical counseling to help improve their overall mental health and well-being. Situations such as depression, post-traumatic stress, and disability can be improved with counseling. In this case, we make available an online tele-mental health group practice called Synergy eTherapy. Counseling with a licensed therapist or health psychologist by phone and/or video chat can be a helpful adjunct treatment along with or after completing your health coaching in this program.

 

Goals of Clinical Counseling

With SYNERGY eTHERAPY, the licensed therapist will help you:

Learn coping skills that can help reduce impairing levels of anxiety, depression or other clinical symptoms

Process past and/or current traumas, negative experiences, or conflicts that have caused you significant distress

Facilitate change using a safe, consistent, non-judgmental, and supportive approach

Empower your own choices to engage in self-care by improving your intrinsic self- motivation and energy

Engaging responsibly to inspire and support you to function independently and successfully

Engaging your internal strengths and external resources to achieve sustainable changes in your thoughts, emotions, and lifestyle choices

 

Selecting Your Clinical Counselor with Synergy eTherapy

After contacting Synergy eTherapy, based on your state of residence and your symptoms, you will be matched to a highly-qualified licensed therapist or health psychologist and complete a FREE consultation.

After your FREE consultation and it is decided that online Telemental health therapy with Synergy is a good fit for your clinical needs, you will become a patient and engage by phone and/or video directly with your therapist.

 

The First Visit with your Synergy Therapist

At the first visit, you will complete an intake assessment

Together, you will decide how your licensed therapist can best help you improve your condition

You need to establish goals and work on how best to achieve them

Your therapist will provide a safe, consistent, non-judgmental, and supportive approach at each Telemental health visit
