Change Assessment

 
 

Readiness to Change: How much are you ready to change your lifestyle to improve your pain? (0 to 10 scale with 0-not at all and 10-completely ready)

 

How important is it for you to make changes at this time?

 

How confident are you able to change at this time?

 

How interested are you in making changes?

 

How much will things get in the way or prevent your change?

 

How much do you feel you can succeed in making changes?

 

How much do you believe it is worth the effort?

 

How much do the benefits outweigh the challenges of making these changes

 

Can you picture yourself as this new person if changes are made?

 

How much do you think your family and friends will react positively to these changes?

 

How much will your friends and family help you in your efforts?

Guide to Interpretation

– You are interested in change but may not be ready to make any changes quite yet. – You are considering your goals and what changes you would like to make and the barriers that need to be addressed to accomplish them.
– You are prepared to change and are willing to make the changes and address the barriers to create how you want to be.
– You are ready to learn the new skills and lifestyles necessary to create how you want to be.
